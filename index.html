Bio

I am an applied scientist in the AWS AI team of Amazon. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Virginia Tech under the guidance of Prof. Changwoo Min and Prof. Dongyoon Lee. I received my bachelor and master degree from Sun Yat-Sen University. I interned in the AWS AI team of Amazon in Summer 2021.
